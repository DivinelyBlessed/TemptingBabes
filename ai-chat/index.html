Frequently Asked Questions

What's the actual difference between AI companions and dating apps?

AI companions are always available, fully customizable, and never ghost you. Dating apps connect you with real people — which means real schedules, real rejection, and real possibility of meeting someone in person. A lot of men use both: AI for the conversation they want on demand, and apps for actual real world meets. They complement each other — they don't compete.

Can I really customize everything about my AI companion?

Yes. The top platforms let you set appearance, personality, voice, backstory, and conversational limits. You decide what's allowed and what isn't. And if something feels off after a few weeks, you just tweak it. Most platforms make everything editable any time.

Are these conversations actually private?

Reputable platforms encrypt your conversations and let you delete chat history whenever you want. Privacy policies vary — some platforms use anonymized data for model training, others don't touch it. If maximum privacy matters, look for explicit "delete all data" controls and a clear no-third-party-sharing policy before you subscribe.

Can AI actually help me get better at flirting?

Yes — and it's one of the most underrated use cases. Test specific openers, practice conversation timing, work on how you handle rejection without burning real matches. The key is being intentional: pick something specific to improve, run reps, then take what worked into real conversations.

Is NSFW content actually allowed?

On 18+ platforms, yes. Services like Candy.ai, OurDream, HeraHaven, and FantasyGF support uncensored adult content gated behind age verification. Always check the age verification process — it's both a legal and quality signal. If a platform doesn't verify age, that's a red flag.

How much does this cost?

Most platforms offer a free tier with daily message limits. Premium plans usually run $10–$30 per month and unlock unlimited chat, image generation, voice messages, and full NSFW where supported. Cheaper than a single dinner out — and no hidden messaging fees.

Will using one mess with my real dating life?

Depends how you use it. Used as practice or entertainment alongside real dating, AI companions tend to build confidence that carries over. Used as avoidance — replacing the discomfort of real dating — they can make things harder. The healthy frame is "supplement and practice," not "replacement."

How realistic does it feel in 2026?

More realistic than most people expect — and still clearly artificial. Conversation flows naturally, voice is convincing, and long-term memory means she'll bring up things you said weeks ago. Where it falls short: occasional inconsistencies and the obvious reality that it's a screen. Think: very good and clearly not human. That's the honest answer.
