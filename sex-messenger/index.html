Why we built this

We'd all been there — on the apps, exhausted. Hundreds of matches, barely any conversations. Photos judged in half a second. The feeling of being lonely while "talking" to twelve people at once.

So we made something different. On Evenings, you don't swipe. You read a prompt someone answered, and if it makes you want to respond, you respond. Profiles unlock as you talk. Pictures come later, not first.
