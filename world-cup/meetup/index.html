FAQ
How can I find official World Cup viewing parties in my city?

Check the FIFA official website, local sports news outlets, and city tourism boards. Many host cities will have designated fan zones.

Are there any apps that help connect local football fans?

Yes, apps like Meetup, Facebook Groups, and even some sports-specific social platforms can help you find local fan communities and events.
