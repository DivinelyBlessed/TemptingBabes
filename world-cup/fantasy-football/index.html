Strategy Guide

5 Rules to Win Your Fantasy League

1
Target players from teams with an easy group
A striker against weak opposition scores more fantasy points. Check the draw and find premium attackers with Group A or D fixtures before splashing your budget.
2
Buy cheap defenders from teams with clean sheet probability
Defenders from Morocco, England or France often outperform their price point. Clean sheets in the group stage stack points without requiring goals.
3
Don't captain in the group stage
Save your triple captain chip for the Round of 16 or quarter-finals when your star player faces weaker opposition in a must-win match. Timing is everything.
4
Wildcard after the group stage draw
Use your wildcard once the group stage draw reveals which teams have the easiest knockout paths. Restructure your squad entirely around the new reality of the bracket.
5
One differential pick per team
In a 30+ person league, everyone owns Mbappé and Vinicius. Find one player nobody else owns who has the potential for a 20+ point game week. That's where leagues are won.

The official FIFA Fantasy World Cup 2026 game launches approximately 4 weeks before the tournament. FotMob and Sky Sports also run excellent unofficial versions with slightly different scoring structures. Play multiple games simultaneously if you want to build a full picture of which picks are delivering value across platforms.
