FAQ
How important is a coach's role in the World Cup?

A coach's role is paramount. They are responsible for team selection, tactics, player motivation, and in-game management, all of which are crucial for success in a tournament setting.

Do coaches often change during the World Cup?

While rare, a coach might be replaced during a tournament if a team performs exceptionally poorly. However, most coaching changes happen before or after major tournaments.

Successful World Cup coaches often possess strong tactical acumen, excellent man-management skills, the ability to adapt to different opponents, and the capacity to inspire their players under immense pressure.
