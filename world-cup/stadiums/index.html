The most historically significant football stadium on earth. Home to Pelé's 1970 World Cup masterclass, Maradona's Hand of God and Goal of the Century in 1986, and now the opening match of 2026. The only stadium to host three World Cups. The altitude creates a unique playing environment — visiting teams struggle for the first 20 minutes as lungs adjust to the thinner air. Home nations benefit enormously from this advantage.
