Buying Guide

How to Get Official Tickets

All official World Cup 2026 tickets are sold exclusively through FIFA.com/tickets. FIFA operates a ballot system for high-demand matches — you apply during sales windows and are randomly selected. Group stage tickets for less prominent matches (non-USA, non-Brazil, non-host nation games) are often available in later sale phases without a ballot. The Final, semi-finals and host nation matches all require ballot entry.

The FIFA ticket application process requires creating a FIFA account, verifying your identity, and linking a valid payment method before applying for tickets. Applications can be submitted during officially announced sales windows only. Late applications go into a waitlist that is occasionally activated when cancelled tickets are returned. Set FIFA.com calendar alerts for the official sales window announcements well before the tournament.

⚠️
Only Buy from FIFA.com
Third-party ticket sites carry significant fraud risk. FIFA's official platform is the only guaranteed source of authentic tickets with valid QR entry codes.
🔄
Resale — FIFA's Platform
FIFA operates a licensed resale exchange where holders can return tickets at face value. This is the safest secondary market and tickets appear regularly as travel plans change.
💳
Multiple Cards Ready
During high-demand sales windows, payment can fail under load. Have two payment methods ready and try different browsers if checkout fails on first attempt.
📅
Target Final Round Dates
Group stage tickets in the final round of games (when standings matter) are more expensive but create the most dramatic atmosphere. Target these over opening group stage games.
