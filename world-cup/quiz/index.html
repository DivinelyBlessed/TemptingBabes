WORLD CUP 2026 QUIZ

10 questions on World Cup history, the 2026 tournament and the competing nations. Answers revealed below each question.

Q1
How many teams compete in the 2026 FIFA World Cup "” and why is it different from previous editions?
48 teams "” up from 32. The expanded format includes more nations from CONCACAF, CAF, AFC and OFC for the first time.
Q2
Which stadium hosts the 2026 World Cup Final?
MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, New Jersey "” the largest capacity venue in the entire tournament.
Q3
Who are the defending World Cup champions entering 2026?
Argentina "” who defeated France on penalties in the epic 2022 Qatar final. Messi's crowning moment.
Q4
Which African nation reached the World Cup semi-finals for the very first time in 2022?
Morocco "” stunning European giants Portugal and Spain on the way to a historic semi-final.
Q5
Who holds the all-time record for most World Cup goals in history?
Miroslav Klose (Germany) "” 16 goals across four World Cups from 2002 to 2014.
Q6
Brazil has won the World Cup how many times "” more than any other nation?
Five times: 1958, 1962, 1970, 1994 and 2002. Germany and Italy have four each.
Q7
How many total matches are played in the 2026 World Cup?
104 matches "” significantly up from 64 in the previous 32-team format.
Q8
Which of these US cities is NOT a host city for 2026: Chicago, Dallas, or Philadelphia?
Chicago "” not among the 11 US host cities. Dallas (AT&T Stadium) and Philadelphia both host group stage and knockout matches.
Q9
The opening ceremony for the 2026 World Cup takes place in which city?
Mexico City "” at the iconic Estadio Azteca, hosting a World Cup for an unprecedented third time.
Q10
Which player is the all-time leading scorer in World Cup history for a single tournament with 13 goals?
Just Fontaine (France) "” scored 13 goals in the 1958 World Cup, a record that has stood for over 65 years.
