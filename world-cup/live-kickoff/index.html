The 2026 World Cup deliberately scheduled most group stage matches at 12PM, 3PM, 6PM and 9PM Eastern Time to maximise the US television audience. This means UK fans face a run of matches kicking off between 5PM and 2AM BST — manageable for the earlier games, brutal for the late ones. Australian fans will catch the 9PM ET games at a reasonable lunchtime slot.
